How to use Illumni

First is go to our homepage, this is where you can locate different categories you can visit. Simply click them to proceed

After Clicking a category, you will be sent to our bookshelf that has the corresponding books, related to the category you picked.

Scroll down to find different selection of books we have in the bookshelf and click a book you want to read.

After choosing a book, you will be sent to the description page of the book where it has the title, author, short description, its genre, date, and etc.

Simply click "Start your imagination" to start reading the book.

for every webpages you visit, there will be a "go back" button on the corner right of your screen. This allows you to go back to the previous page you were on.
